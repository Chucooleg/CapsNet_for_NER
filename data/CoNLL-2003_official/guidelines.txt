* Style guidelines and example paper can be found at 
  http://cnts.uia.ac.be/conll2003/ner/
* Send the files to erikt@uia.ua.ac.be
* Please include the following in your submission:
  1. camera-ready version of the paper (ps or pdf)
  2. Latex source (including references) or plain text version
  3. result files for the both development sets and both test sets.
* The final results of your method should be displayed in four 
  tables in the LEFT column of the final page of the paper. The
  overall results should be put in the BOTTOM of each table. See
  the example in http://cnts.uia.ac.be/conll2003/ner/example.pdf

  string "CoNLL-2003" (notice the hyphen and the complete year).

  overview paper:
